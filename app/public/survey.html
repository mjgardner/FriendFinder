FriendFinder Compatibility Survey

  2. You often get so lost in thoughts that you ignore or forget your surroundings.
  3. You try to respond to your e-mails as soon as possible and cannot stand a messy inbox.
  4. You find it easy to stay relaxed and focused even when there is some pressure.
  5. You don’t usually initiate conversations.
  6. You feel a constant need for something new.
  7. You feel it is better to regret something you did than something you did not do.
  8. Being adaptable is more important to you than being organized.
  9. You believe that other people’s misfortune is not your problem.
  10. Winning a debate is more important to you than making sure no one gets upset.
